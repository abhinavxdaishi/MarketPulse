Essential Financial Disclaimer

MarketPulse is an educational and analytical portal. We do not provide personalized financial, investment, or trading advice. The analysis, charting patterns, and research commentaries published here are for general informational purposes only. Trading equities, options, and cryptocurrencies carries an extremely high risk of capital loss. Past performance is in no way predictive of future market results. Consult a certified financial advisor before committing real capital to any trades.

1. Data We Access Locally

MarketPulse operates under a highly optimized, server-less metadata design. We do not transmit your local inputs to any central data center. Any actions you perform—such as:

• Setting your theme preference (Dark Mode or Light Mode)
• Posting commentaries on stock analysis briefings
• Drafting and submitting custom financial briefings

Are stored strictly in your browser's private segment called Local Storage (`localStorage`). This data remains on your machine and can be cleared at any time by emptying your browser cache.

2. Analytical Accuracy & Tickers

All stock quotes, volume readings, and ticker summaries displayed on this website are static representations or delayed by at least 15 minutes. We make no warranty as to the completeness or immediate accuracy of order flows, short interest percentages, or option chains reported herein.

3. Social Media & External Integrations

We do not track you using cookies or third-party analytical nodes. The social links located in the header and footer (Twitter, GitHub, YouTube, LinkedIn) are outgoing anchors and do not exchange browser metrics with our static server.

4. Updates to This Policy

As financial regulations and local browser security rules evolve, we may modify this static policy. Re-reading this page upon subsequent visits ensures you remain aligned with our disclosure parameters.
